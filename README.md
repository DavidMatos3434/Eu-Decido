## Vision

EU DECIDO is an experimental civic technology initiative exploring new forms of democratic participation through open technologies, transparent governance mechanisms, and responsible artificial intelligence.

The project aims to create digital infrastructures that allow citizens to:
- participate continuously in democratic processes,
- propose and debate ideas,
- vote transparently and securely,
- and collectively shape governance structures.

EU DECIDO combines:
- digital participation,
- anonymous voting systems,
- community deliberation,
- and future multi-agent AI architectures aligned with European values and Responsible AI principles.
